üìú Code of Conduct

1. Respect Everyone
Be kind, considerate, and respectful. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind. Zero tolerance.
2. No Spam
Avoid spamming messages, emojis, or links. Keep the data stream clean and flowing naturally.
3. No Drama
Keep personal conflicts private or off-platform. This space is for building and gaming, not fighting.
4. Keep Channels On-Topic
Post content where it belongs. Don't post Minecraft memes in the serious dev forums.
5. No Self-Promotion
Only share personal projects if explicitly permitted by Admins or in designated "Showcase" areas.
6. No NSFW Content
No age-restricted or obscene content. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, extreme violence, or disturbing content.
7. Appropriate Language
Avoid excessive profanity. Slurs are an instant ban.
